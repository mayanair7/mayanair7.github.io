Frequently Asked Questions

  • 1

    Why choose me as your child's Maths Tutor?

    I strongly believe that my age allows me to connect closely with my students. I teach my students methods, tips and techniques that I was taught not long ago, from the top set of one of the UK's leading schools. I am able to pass on the very tools I used and applied in order to achieve the highest grades possible.

    As a tutor, I am patient, thorough and engaging, bringing a genuine love and enthusiasm for teaching, learning and mathematics to each lesson.

  • 2

    What experience do I have?

    I have been tutoring since I was 16 years old. I have taught students aged 9 up to 18 years old. In addition to this, during my Gap Year, I taught Maths to Monks in Sri Lanka. The language barrier forced me to adapt to more abstract ways of teaching, which I have carried through to my teaching here.

  • 3

    How do I carry out my lessons?

    I tutor online and in person. When tutoring online, I usually use zoom but am flexible to your preferred platform. I write on my iPad, sharing it's screen and my face simultaneously. This allows for a lesson as similar as possible to face to face learning. I find that this experience aids my students' learning greatly.

  • 4

    What is the structure of a lesson?

    I tailor my teaching style to suit your child's needs.

    If your child is struggling with specific questions, I like to go over the topic with them to make sure there are no gaps in their understanding. I then work through the questions with the student, providing similar ones for homework to consolidate their learning.

    I also teach the students' specification from scratch (or a point of their choosing), making notes on my iPad which I send to them at the end of each lesson. I provide plenty of questions throughout and after lessons to consolidate their understanding.
